About

Building platforms that generate revenue and eliminate toil

I am a Senior Technical PM who operates at the intersection of API monetization, developer experience, and AI-powered automation. My work centers on one question: how do you make complex systems so simple that non-technical users can run them without engineering help?

At SquareTrade (Allstate Protection Plans), I own platform and developer experience for one of the largest warranty and protection plan providers in North America, processing 50M+ transactions annually across Walmart, Target, Home Depot, and 80+ global retail partners.

Before that, I spent four years at DISH Network building smart home ecosystem integrations with Google Nest, Ring, and Arlo, and designing the matching algorithm that drove a 20% lift in same-day installation revenue.

I hold an MBA from CU Boulder (Leeds Business Scholarship), an MS in IT and Management from UT Dallas, and a BS in Computer Engineering from the University of Pune.
